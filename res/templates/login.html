Welcome to Rhizi!

Rhizi is a collaborative knowledge editor. We are using it to create an interactome of AIV M2 students, their skills, interests, clubs and internships.

This version is meant for you to add yourself in the interactome and share how it went.

Send us an Email about your experience, especially if you encountered any problem!
