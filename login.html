Rhizi sandbox login

Rhizi is a collaborative knowledge editor. With it, you can jointly define, explore and visualise projects, communities and ideas.

This test version is meant for you to try Rhizi out and provide us feedback so AIV students and teachers will have a good experience using it once we deploy Jan 1st.

Try it out by doing the following things:

  • Login with your guest account
  • Create a node with your name.
  • Connect to your node three scientific interests you have(e.g. Systems biology, Biochemistry, Quantum physics, etc')
  • Connect to your node three skills you know well(e.g. Javascript, Photoshop, People management, Financing etc')
  • Send us an Email about your experience, especially if you encountered any problem!

Out of scope

  • Content on this sandbox may be deleted at any time.
  • You can export your work as a JSON, but we don't guarantee we will continue to support it later on.
  • We will implement a feature to generate you own Rhizi-doc in May. Until then, you will not be able to launch your own Rhizi-doc.
